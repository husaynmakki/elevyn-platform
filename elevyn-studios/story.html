Our Story

Designed on the same floor it's worn on.

Elevyn Studios started as a rack of member requests inside Elevyn Wellness Elevated in Dearborn — a heavier tee, a warmer layer for the walk to the car, leggings that didn't feel like armor. It's grown into a full apparel line, still fit-tested by the members and trainers who asked for it first.
